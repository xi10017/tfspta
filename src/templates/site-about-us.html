One PTA serving both campuses. All Frazer middle and high school families and staff are welcome.

Shared mission

We partner with Frazer School staff and families to fund programs, organize events, and advocate for resources that help students at both the middle school and high school thrive.

Middle School

Membership

Annual dues: $10 per family. Supports grants, field trips, and student activities at the middle school.

Meetings

Second Tuesday of each month, 7:00 PM in the middle school cafeteria. Check the MS newsletter for childcare details.

What we fund

  • Classroom supplies and teacher wish lists
  • Assemblies and enrichment programs
  • Family nights and grade-level events
  • Club and activity support

High School

Membership

Annual dues: $10 per family. Supports senior events, athletics, arts, and student leadership programs.

Meetings

Third Tuesday of each month, 7:00 PM in the high school library. Agendas are posted on the HS parent portal.

What we fund

  • Senior class and graduation support
  • Arts, music, and theater programs
  • Athletic and spirit events
  • Scholarship and student recognition
